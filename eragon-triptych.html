Eragon Triptych

Eragon is a book by Christopher Paolini. This piece was created for the Jones family.

Eragon Triptych - Left Sketch

Eragon Triptych - Center Sketch

Eragon is forging his own sword after completing his training as a dragon rider. As he names it Bresingr, it bursts into blue flame.
