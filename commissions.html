Work With Karen Swan

Creating art for someone else is one of Karen’s favorite things to do. She loves the chance to step into someone’s story and bring something personal, meaningful, and lasting to life—something you will treasure in your own home or give as a gift to someone you love.

For her, this has never been about the money. It is about making something beautiful for another person. If all you had to offer was a song, then a song would be the price.

That said, each piece takes an immense amount of time, care, and attention. Every commission starts with a conversation so she can understand your idea and create something truly special.

Submit a request below to start the conversation, and include as much information as you can about what you are looking for, timing, dimensions, style, inspiration, or reference ideas.
