About the Artist

Learn more about Karen Swan, her artistic voice, and the work behind the collection.

Karen Swan is an artist whose work reflects a thoughtful eye for composition, color, and emotional texture. Her pieces explore visual storytelling through a personal and expressive lens, balancing detail, mood, and experimentation.

Across projects, her work moves between polished finished pieces and more exploratory studies, always with a clear artistic voice. This site gathers those works into a single archive so visitors can experience the range and continuity of her creative process.

Her work is rooted in curiosity, observation, and a desire to create pieces that feel both visually engaging and personally meaningful.
